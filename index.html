Description

The aim of this conference is to join together international experts and young researchers alike, with a view on exploring noncommutative geometry in its many varied incarnations. Particular emphasis is placed on the vast range of topics cultivated and enriched by Michel Van den Bergh, who we join in celebrating Kanreki in 2020.
